About Sameeha

A student leader who listens first.

My Leadership Journey

From class representative to house committee volunteer, my journey has been about showing up for classmates — organising study groups, resolving small conflicts, and making sure quieter voices in the room get heard too.

Why I Want to Serve

Our school gave me more than lessons — it gave me confidence. As SPL, I want to return that by building a campus where every student feels supported to learn, create, and grow, and where good ideas from any classroom can actually become real changes.
