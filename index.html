About Me

Sameeha.S

Biography

I am Sameeha.S, a dedicated and passionate student at SRC Memorial Matric Higher Secondary School. Throughout my academic journey, I have demonstrated a commitment to excellence, compassion for my peers, and a deep love for learning. I believe that leadership is not about standing above others — it is about standing beside them and lifting them higher.

Mission

To create an inclusive, vibrant school environment where every student feels seen, heard, and empowered to reach their full potential — through knowledge, service, and unity.

Vision

A school where leadership is not a privilege but a shared responsibility — where teachers, students, and staff write a better chapter together, every single day.
